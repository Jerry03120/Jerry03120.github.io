I am a Postdoctoral Research Fellow in the Department of Civil and Environmental Engineering at the University of Houston, working with Dr. Abigail L. Beck. My research focuses on applying machine learning to improve the resilience of critical infrastructure — particularly power grids — against extreme weather events. I completed my Ph.D. in Civil Engineering at Texas A&M University under Dr. Stephanie G. Paal, and interned at Oak Ridge National Laboratory as part of the Grid Resilience Research Group.

Research Interests

  • ML for Critical Infrastructure Resilience
  • Power Grid Analytics & Forecasting
  • Explainable AI for Decision Support
  • LiDAR & Machine Vision
  • Community Resilience & Big Data
  • LLMs for Emergency Planning

  • Deployed near-real-time prediction systems; applied transfer learning for data-sparse hazard scenarios.

Focus Areas

Research

Three threads, one mission: decision-ready intelligence for vulnerable infrastructure.

Power Outage Modeling

Generalized and near-real-time prediction of electricity disruptions during winter storms and multi-hazard extreme events across nine U.S. states. Inputs include meteorological, geographical, and socio-demographic data with Bayesian-tuned ensemble models and SHAP explainability.

XGBoostLightGBMTransfer LearningSHAP

Machine Vision & LiDAR

Automated assessment of coastal utility pole deterioration and tilt angle measurement using Grounded SAM 3, semantic segmentation, and LiDAR point cloud integration. Human-in-the-loop validation workflows for real-world deployment.

Grounded SAMCloudComparePoint CloudCNN

Community Resilience

Integrated spatiotemporal assessment of infrastructure resilience and community vulnerability across Houston using 311 service requests, big data analytics, census data, and geospatial context to quantify neighborhood-level outage impact.

GeoPandasQGIS311 DataSpatial Stats

Engineering Resilience
through Data Science.

I am a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at the University of Houston. My work bridges the gap between Civil Engineering and Advanced Data Analytics.

I specialize in developing interpretable machine learning frameworks to predict infrastructure failures and enhance community resilience against extreme climate events. My expertise spans large-scale power outage modeling, LiDAR data fusion, and explainable AI (XAI).

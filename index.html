About Me

I am a Postdoctoral Research Fellow in the Department of Civil and Environmental Engineering at the University of Houston. My research develops comprehensive data-driven frameworks for infrastructure resilience by integrating multi-source data such as LiDAR, machine vision, and meteorological records. Previously, I conducted power grid resilience analysis at Oak Ridge National Laboratory (ORNL) during Summer 2024 as part of the GRO Program (Internship).

Education

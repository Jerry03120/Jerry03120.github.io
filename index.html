Building Resilient Infrastructure
through Data Science.

I am a Postdoctoral Research Fellow in the Department of Civil and Environmental Engineering at the University of Houston, working with Dr. Abigail L. Beck.

My research operates at the intersection of civil engineering and data science. I develop comprehensive data-driven frameworks for infrastructure and community resilience assessment by integrating multi-source data—ranging from LiDAR and machine vision to meteorological datasets.

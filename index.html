About Me

I am a Postdoctoral Research Fellow in the Department of Civil and Environmental Engineering at the University of Houston. My research focuses on developing comprehensive data-driven frameworks for infrastructure and community resilience assessment by integrating multi-source data (LiDAR, machine vision, meteorological data, etc.). Previously, I served as a Research Intern at Oak Ridge National Laboratory (ORNL), conducting national-scale power grid resilience analysis.

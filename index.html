Editorial Note 01 / Introduction

I am a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at the University of Houston, standing at the convergence of civil engineering and advanced data analytics.

My work is not merely about predicting outages; it is about architectural foresight. Using LiDAR, machine vision, and extreme climate modeling, I design the frameworks that keep our cities standing when nature strikes its hardest. From the Grid Resilience group at Oak Ridge National Laboratory to the doctoral labs of Texas A&M, my career has been dedicated to one question: How do we make the built environment unbreakable?
